1. A LITTLE ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Alex! I'm a front-end web developer with an eye for design. I build beautiful web experiences and user interfaces that perform seamlessly on any device.

I do not use JQuery or Bootstrap unless they are already part of a project's stack. For most of my front-end projects, I start with good ol' vanilla JS, and add shiny front-end tools like React, Redux, and Webpack where necessary.

I believe the best tool for the job is not the coolest stack but the one that fits the needs of a task with minimal headaches.
